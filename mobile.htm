Why is this here?

Some time ago, after HP acquired Palm, Palm's own "Mobile Portal" page (which is the default homepage for Blazer and a permanent residence of the user's bookmarks) went kaput, to be replaced by a WebOS-centric page that is *definitely* not Blazer-friendly.
What, then, is a Palm OS-hugger to do?

Enter this page. I figured that there are still a few people hanging on tightly to their older handhelds, and who might be despairing over the increasing number of "mobile" sites that just don't feel mobile-friendly on these devices.
Heck, I'm one of those people, and it's out of my league to purchase a newer device just to get with the times (though I often wish I could).
That's why this page is dedicated to being a collection of "starting" sites that are still nice and viewable on these older handheld browers.

Of course, this list is just getting started, so please drop me an email if you have a suggestion on something to add!
